## 2. Target Industries & Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

eWards serves brands across these verticals:

- **Fashion & Apparel Retail** — clothing chains, ethnic wear, party wear, accessories, footwear
- **F&B / Restaurants & Cafes** — fine dining, QSRs, cafes, pubs, bakeries, ice cream chains
- **Jewelry & Watches** — luxury retail, watch stores
- **Shopping Malls & Department Stores** — multi-brand outlets, malls
- **Lifestyle & Home** — home furnishings, family stores
- **Beauty, Salon & Spa** — via partner integrations
- **Electronics & General Retail** — any brand with repeat customers and a POS system
- **International Brands** — eWards operates across multiple countries

**ICP Summary for Lead Qualification:**
- Walk-in customer businesses (physical stores)
- Repeat purchase behavior
- POS system in place (or planning to implement one)
- Multi-outlet operations preferred but not required

**Who within a brand uses eWards:**
- Marketing / CRM Managers
- Store Operations Managers
- Business Owners / Founders
- Digital Marketing Teams
- Finance teams (for fraud module, revenue reports)

## 3. Product Suite

### 3.1 ACE (Customer Relationship & Engagement Management)

**nGine — Loyalty & Rewards Engine**
- Multi-layer tier-based loyalty programs (e.g., Insider → Elite → Ambassador)
- Flat %, visit-based, milestone, SKU-based, billing-amount-based loyalty structures
- Referral programs, eWallet / credit management
- Membership packages (paid memberships with exclusive perks)
- Coupon booklets, credit booklets, recurring booklets
- Configurable earning ratios and validity periods

**nGage — Omnichannel Campaign Engine**
- Auto Engagement campaigns (triggered by events: birthdays, anniversaries, credits expiry)
- Instant Gratification campaigns (triggered by activities: add member, check-in, bill settlement)
- Instant Campaigns (one-time broadcast to specific segments)
- Referral & Social Media Campaigns
- Channels: SMS, WhatsApp (carousel, media, buttons, DLT compliance), Email, Push Notifications
- ROCC (Return on Campaign Cost) tracking

**nHance — Marketing Consultancy Services**
- Campaign research & ideation, designing & executing campaigns
- Data-driven insights, monthly strategy discussions, A/B testing

**nAlytics — Business Analytics**
- Real-time business performance, SKU-level analytics
- Multi-outlet comparison, heat maps, RFM analytics, retention cohorts

### 3.2 Prism (Business Intelligence Tool)
- Real-time insights, data visualizations, forecast trends, mobile dashboard, predictive forecasting

### 3.3 InsightX (AI-Powered CRM Intelligence Layer)
- Analyzes customer data monthly, detects behavioral shifts
- Delivers actionable marketing direction — no manual analysis needed
- Month Navigator: Overview, Focus Brief, Segment Planner, Active/At-Risk/Lost Playbooks

### 3.4 Digital Invoice (nVoice)
- POS-integrated digital receipts, NPS & feedback, customer data capture
- Banner space for promotions, bill verification, social media redirection

### 3.5 Loop (Feedback Module)
- Real-time feedback via QR/link/in-store tabs
- Instant escalation for negative feedback, sentiment analysis
- Integrated CRM — feedback flows into customer profiles

### 3.6 Ping (eCommerce App/Website)
- Personalized eCommerce website, white label app (iOS & Android)
- Integrated CRM & Loyalty, inventory management

## New Company Discovery

For funnel types where no curated list exists (New POS, Channel Distributors, Individual Referrals), the skill should **discover companies via web search** using queries like:

**New POS:**
- "POS software companies in {city}"
- "restaurant POS providers {city}"
- "retail billing software {city}"
- "point of sale companies India {city}"

**Third-Party Vendors (beyond the 5 listed):**
- "retail marketing agencies {city}"
- "F&B consulting companies {city}"
- "Shopify agencies India {city}"
- "franchise consultants {city}"
- "retail technology vendors {city}"

**Channel Distributors:**
- "retail distribution companies {city}"
- "F&B distribution {city}"
- "FMCG distributors with retail clients {city}"

**Qualification for discovered companies:**
- Must serve Retail, F&B, or related verticals
- Their clients should overlap with eWards' ICP
- Prefer 2+ years in business
- Should have direct client relationships (not purely B2C)
- Cross-check: NOT already in the Current POS or Third-Party lists (to avoid miscategorizing)

| Instant Gratification (IG) | Triggered rewards based on activities |
| RFM | Recency, Frequency, Monetary analysis |
| ROCC | Return on Campaign Cost |
| CLM | Customer Lifecycle Marketing |
